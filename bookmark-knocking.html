Bookmark Knocking

Enterprise-grade security tool to hide sensitive URLs. This system generates a sequence of encrypted JavaScript bookmarklets. You must click them in the exact order (the "knock sequence") to unlock and decrypt your hidden site. Data never leaves your browser.

Installation

Drag the generated buttons below to your browser's bookmark bar. They must be clicked in the specified order.
